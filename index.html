About

Professional cinematographer and visual artist with extensive experience in independent film production. Based between New York and Tel Aviv, I work across Camera and Electrical Department, Additional Crew, and Editorial Department roles, bringing stories to life through compelling visual narratives.

With over 15 years in cinema production, I specialize in creating authentic, character-driven cinematography that serves the story while maintaining technical excellence.
